KEY CONNECTORS
24-PIN ATX
Main motherboard power. Required for any boot.
8-PIN EPS
CPU power connector (4+4 or 8-pin). High-end boards may use two.
PCIe 6+2 PIN
GPU auxiliary power. Each connector carries up to 150W.
12VHPWR
New ATX 3.0 connector for high-TDP GPUs. Up to 600W.
SATA POWER
SSDs, HDDs, and accessories (RGB, fans).
MOLEX 4-PIN
Legacy peripheral connector. Still found on some fans and devices.
PSU FAILURE SYMPTOMS
  • Random system restarts or sudden shutdowns
  • PC won't POST or fails to power on
  • System freezes under load (GPU or CPU intensive)
  • Unusual buzzing, whining, or clicking from PSU
  • Components getting unusually hot (voltage sag)
  • Intermittent USB drops or peripheral failures
  • BSOD events tied to power-related stop codes
